How Can My School Sign Up?

  • Are you a middleschool?
  • Are you interested in starting a math club?
  • Do you have a teacher who can be the club advisor?
  • Are you excited to get your students involved in math tournaments?
    • If so, you're ready for Falcon's Math Club.
    Contact us to get started.

    How Can I Become a Coach?

  • Are you a highschool or undergraduate student?
  • Are you passionate about math? (Algebra, Number Theory, and Geometry)
  • Are you free one day a week to teach?
  • Are you looking for volunteer hours?
    • If so, contact us to see if you qualify.
